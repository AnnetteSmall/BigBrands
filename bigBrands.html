About Us

Big Brands offers the first and unique distribution to premium brands in South Africa. On one hand, we're able to leverage on the strengh of Super Group drawing on its established muscle, as one of South Africa's largest distribution companies. This include logistics prowess ans experiance, warehousing capabilities, financial, sales and managerial backing as well as it's large-scale footprint.


Yet Big Brands still shows the passion, experience, hands-on agility and the flexibility of a keen start-up


Combine the two and it's clear why Big Brands is a uniquely powerful brand builder and partner. Our small yet savvy team brings creativity, innovation, dedication and a can-do, won't-stop, roll-up-your-sleeves mindset to all the brands we build, case by case, as if our own. You may ask why?

The answer is simple, because we love brands.
